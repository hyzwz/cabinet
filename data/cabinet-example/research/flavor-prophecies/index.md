# Flavor Prophecies

Notes from tasting sessions, overheard customer opinions, and one suspiciously accurate prediction from Luna.

## Best Signals

- Bright citrus still wins when paired with a dramatic visual effect.
- Adults claim they want "elegant sweets" and then immediately buy the exploding one.
- Gold foil improves perceived value by a frankly irritating amount.

## Quotes Worth Keeping

- "It tastes like a celebration and a minor school rule violation." - Lee Jordan
- "I trust anything that glows politely." - Luna Lovegood
- "No." - Hermione Granger, before buying two boxes anyway
