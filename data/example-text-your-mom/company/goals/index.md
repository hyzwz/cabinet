# Company Goals

## Q2 2026

- Reach 50,000 monthly active users
- Maintain 35% week-4 retention for new users
- Lift free-to-paid conversion from 3.5% to 5%
- Publish a repeatable TikTok and Reddit growth engine
- Ship onboarding v2 focused on first meaningful setup in under 90 seconds

## This Month (April 2026)

- Tighten the growth message around "reply before guilt spirals"
- Reduce reminder setup friction
- Improve bug triage and release discipline

## CEO Reality Check (April 12)

50K MAU by end of Q2 requires ~2.7x growth from 18,400 in roughly 10 weeks. That is aggressive but possible IF:

1. Marketing cabinets activate this week (not next month)
2. Onboarding v2 ships within 3 weeks and measurably improves activation
3. At least one content channel starts producing consistent volume by end of April

If marketing stays paused through April, the 50K target becomes unrealistic. We should revisit the number at the April 26 check-in rather than pretending we are on track.

**Leading indicators to watch weekly:**
- Number of TikTok posts published
- Number of Reddit engagements (comments, not lurking)
- P1 story velocity (stories completed per week)
- Activation rate trend (weekly cohorts)
